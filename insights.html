Mission

In a time where the products that we consume and use everyday are made by companies all around the world, connecting with businesses in our local community is vital to their survival.

Our Solution

One of the largest problems facing small and local businesses all around our communities is reaching customers. By providing a platform where businesses can connect with each other and their customers with a lower investment they will be able to expand their operations and outreach. As a bonus customers have an outlet to find new shops and services that they may have never found.

The Community

The business landscape is constantly changing and we have moved into an age where nearly anything is at our fingertips using online shopping. This has been a huge positive as it makes our everyday lives easier but has come to the detriment of community which seems to be lacking. By giving these businesses a platform to connect with the customers who need their products it is possible to bring that sense of community back.
